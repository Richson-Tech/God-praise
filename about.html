About Our Ministry

Find Out More About GOD PRAISE MINISTRY Church. Learn More About What We Believe, How We Got Here, What Our Mission Is, And How We Serve The Kingdom.

Journalist John Edward provides a helpful study of this phenomenal ministry in his book “Ministry of Life" The Extraordinary Growth of the "The God Praise Ministry”, after talking with many members of the church who told him how Christ has miraculously intervened and changed lives in response to prayer and the proclamation of the Gospel. In Pastor Richson’s own words: “God, in the growth of"The God Praise Ministry”, has strategically and prudently used miracles to ‘make all men come to Him’ (John 3:26). We take Christian living and holiness seriously.”
According to Aaron Jacob, The history of "The God Praise Ministry” is very much the personal story of Richson Tech. He is a gifted man, clear-thinking and humble. He is a dedicated Christian and deserves the title ‘the man of God,’ which his followers have given him. Since he was a young man, Richson has devoted his time to reading and studying the Bible. He knows the Scriptures inside out, and has struggled to understand and apply them. As he shares that struggle and his love for the word of God, so people have been drawn to him for advice, or simply to listen. When he preaches, his message is clear, simple, and profound. Poor street traders who can barely read sit next to university professors, all equally captivated by the way Scripture becomes suddenly relevant to them.
